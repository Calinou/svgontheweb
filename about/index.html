About

This guide accompanies a talk given by Jake Giltsoff at Breaking Borders on the 16th of June 2015. It outlines some techniques you can use to prepare, implement, and work with SVGs on the web. Anything to add or change? Fork this on Github. If you would like Jake to talk about SVGs for your team or event, get in touch.

I’ll do my best to keep the page updated and running on the web as a reference you can come back to when you need it.

The examples on the page use the Breaking Borders logo, with permission from Ben MacGowan — thanks Ben!
